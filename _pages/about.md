I am currently a first-year Master student in the Department of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Nanyang Ye. I also maintain close collaboration with Prof. Cewu Lu’s research group.

My primary research interests are embodied intelligence and VLA models, and I am also interested in applications of VLMs/LLMs. I hope to advance embodied intelligence toward achieving human-level intelligence.

Previously, I earned my bachelor’s degree at Nanjing University. In 2024 I completed an internship at Huawei, where I mainly participated in the development of animation components for the HarmonyOS operating system.

目前我是上海交通大学计算机系的硕士一年级学生，由叶南阳老师指导，同时我也与卢策吾老师课题组保持紧密合作。

我的主要研究兴趣主要包括具身智能和VLA模型，同时我也对VLM/LLM的应用感兴趣。我希望能推动具身智能实用化发展，达到人类级别的智能水平。

此前我在南京大学取得了本科学位。2024年我曾在华为有一段实习经历，主要参与鸿蒙操作系统动效组件的开发。
